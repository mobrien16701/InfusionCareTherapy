Why IV Therapy

A faster path to feeling better.

Oral supplements lose much of their potency in digestion. Our IV infusions and intramuscular injections deliver vitamins, minerals, amino acids and other supplements directly into the bloodstream — for immediate availability, faster results and a more predictable clinical outcome where your body needs them most.

Absorption

By injecting directly into the blood vessel, nutrients by-pass the digestive processes with a significantly higher degree of saturation to the cell. The other benefit is feeling the effects of IV nutrients much faster than traditional methods.

Clinically Formulated

Every drip and injection are prepared and administered by licensed clinicians — safe, sterile, and tailored to your goals and or prescribed by your doctor.

Relief in About 60 Minutes

Most infusions take about an hour. Relax in our serene treatment suite — and walk out feeling restored.
